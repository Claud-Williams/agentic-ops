What else is in the system

safe-apply

A git-backed safety floor that makes overnight autonomy safe rather than scary: every change is verified and one step from undo.

scrub-gate

A publish-time scanner that blocks secrets, personal data and home-machine paths from ever reaching a public repository.

scheduler

A plain-text overnight job runner: read-only or act mode, with retry and honest reporting.

principal-modelling

Learns how a person thinks, can act with their judgement, and actively sharpens it over time.

self-healing-audit

Audits its own rules for drift on a trigger and repairs the safe ones, earning autonomy gradually.

handoff-architecture

How several agents across devices stay in sync: read everyone's state, write only your own.

calibration-method

Logs predicted versus actual effort and feeds the bands back, so estimates correct themselves.

The full write-ups and source for each component live in this repository's README and folders. Sample data throughout uses a fictional persona and resembles no real person or organisation.
